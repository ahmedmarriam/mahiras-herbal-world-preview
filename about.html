"For Mahira, healing isn't a product. It's a responsibility."

Mahira Shafi's journey began in childhood, learning about herbs alongside her herbalist father — a foundation that instilled a lifelong belief in the healing power of nature.

A Voice That Made History

Before dedicating herself fully to herbal wellness, Mahira built an extraordinary media career. She joined Pakistan Television (PTV) as a newscaster and Radio Pakistan as a program host — where her clarity, poise and presence set her apart immediately.

Within just one month of joining PTV, she was honoured with the prestigious Bolan Award. She made history as the first non-celebrity to be featured on the cover of Akhbar-e-Jahan, and was among the first broadcasters to deliver full news bulletins entirely from memory — without a teleprompter.

Educator, Scholar & Broadcaster

Holding a Master's degree in English and professional teaching certifications, Mahira became one of the youngest teachers at the Convent of Jesus and Mary, Murree — where her students included Benazir Bhutto and children of Pakistan's most distinguished families.

She created and hosted a nationwide "School Broadcast Program" on Radio Pakistan, designing the full curriculum and teaching English grammar to matric-level students across the country. She later wrote award-winning articles for the Saudi Gazette.

From Legacy to Purpose

A certified herbalist and devoted mother of five, Mahira channelled a lifetime of knowledge, care and experience into Mahira's Herbal World — bringing the healing power of herbs to you.
