The Founder

Rooted in Tradition.
Guided by Knowledge.

"For Mahira, healing isn't a product. It's a responsibility."

Mahira Shafi's journey began in childhood, learning about herbs alongside her herbalist father. Before dedicating herself to natural wellness, she built an extraordinary media career — joining Pakistan Television (PTV) as a newscaster and Radio Pakistan as a host, earning the Bolan Award within her first month and becoming the first non-celebrity on the cover of Akhbar-e-Jahan.

A Master's graduate in English, she taught at the Convent of Jesus and Mary, Murree — where her students included Benazir Bhutto. She created and hosted a nationwide School Broadcast Program on Radio Pakistan, and later contributed award-winning writing to the Saudi Gazette.

A certified herbalist and devoted mother of five, she transformed a lifetime of knowledge into Mahira's Herbal World.
